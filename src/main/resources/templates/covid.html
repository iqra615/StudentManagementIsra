Covid-19 Press Release

Press Release
Covid 19 screening information and Research Desk to implement SOPS has been established at Main OPD, welfare OPD and Block A of Isra University Hyderabad where temperature. masks wearing, hand hygiene are checked for staff, faculty, Patients, visitors staff, and security personnel and emergency duty doctors .screening facility is also available for undergraduates students and postgraduates students. The Vice Chancellor prof Nazir Ashraf Laghari visited the desk and got his temperature checked on this occasion Prof. Hussain Bux Kolachi Dean Basic Science, was present and talking to house Officers, staff and visitors Prof Nazir Ashraf said that SOPS issued by Sindh Government and Regulatory bodies like HEC and PMDC as well NCOC be followed in the all campuses of Isra University at Karachi ,Islamabad and Hyderabad As corona virus infection is pandemic diseases and more than 200000 cases have been reported in Pakistan since first case was diagnosed on 26th February this year Social distancing. hand washing, mask wearing and avoiding going in crowded places like markets and gathering
Professor Kolachi advise to the community utilizing Isra Health care services from its hospitals and students enrolled to follow SOPs and Practice Preventive measures. Isra has given its Welfare Hospital for to Government of Sindh to be used as Quarantine and isolation Centre Isra University Research and Information and Screening Desk is running awareness campaign through social media and mass media which has reached to more than 10,000 peoples as medical and dental students are also informing people about prevention methods of Corona Virus World Health Organization guidelines and other awareness literature has been distributed to more than 1200 patients and general public .

Dr. Hussain Bux Kolachi
Focal Person COVID -19
Isra University Hyderabad
